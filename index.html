Welcome to Stockholm

The capital of Scandinavia, a city of islands, history, and innovation. Let's discover Stockholm together

Stockholm, the capital of Sweden, encompasses 14 islands and more than 50 bridges on an extensive Baltic Sea archipelago. The cobblestone streets and ochre-colored buildings of Gamla Stan (the Old Town) are home to the 13th-century Storkyrkan Cathedral, the Kungliga Slottet Royal Palace and the Nobel Museum. Ferries and sightseeing boats shuttle passengers between the islands.
